# Nutrition in the follicular phase

After your period, in the follicular phase, your body slowly builds back up and many
people notice more energy. Fresh, light food often fits that nicely — though here too:
eat what feels good, without rules.

This is general information, not medical or nutritional advice.

## Fresh and light

Vegetables, fruit, salads and whole grains feel good to many people in this phase.
Lighter meals suit the fresher, more energetic feeling the follicular phase often
brings.

## Fermented foods

Sauerkraut, kefir, yoghurt or kimchi support your gut in a natural way. A spoon of
fermented food with your meal already counts — no obligation, just a pleasant addition.

## Room for new habits

The rising energy makes this a nice moment to try something new in the kitchen, if you
feel like it. It doesn't have to — listening to your own rhythm comes first.
