# Cycle tracker without an account

Most cycle apps ask you to create an account first. With that, your most personal
information — your period, your mood, your sexual health — ends up on a server you
don't control. It can be different. A **cycle tracker without an account** keeps
everything on your own device, so only you see what you log.

## What does "without an account" mean?

No email, no password, no username. You open the app and start. Because there is no
account, there is also nothing to leak, sell or link to your name. You don't have to
trust anyone with your data, because your data goes nowhere.

## So where does your data live?

With Paced, everything stays **local in your browser or app**, on the device in front
of you. There is no cloud, no analytics and no advertising network watching along.
Concretely that means:

- No accounts and no passwords to lose.
- No ads and no selling your data to third parties.
- Full export to CSV or Apple Health, whenever you want.
- One button to wipe everything — with no hidden backups.

## What can you track?

A privacy-friendly app doesn't have to be bare. In Paced you track your menstrual
cycle, nutrition, sleep, movement, symptoms and mood. The app uses your cycle phase to
add context to what you log — patterns to help you listen to your body, no predictions
and no prescriptions.

## Is a local app less convenient?

It doesn't have to be. You can export your data any time and take it with you, and the
optional partner feature lets you share only your current cycle phase via one link if
you want — your journal and your logs still stay on your own device.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I lose my data if I switch phones?**
Because the data is local, you take it with you via the export (CSV or JSON) and
restore it on your new device.

**Does Paced make money from my data?**
No. There are no ads and no data sales. That is exactly the starting point.
