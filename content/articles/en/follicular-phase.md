# The follicular phase

The follicular phase begins after your period and runs up to ovulation. It is the
build-up phase of your cycle: your body slowly prepares for a possible ovulation.
Many people experience this as a lighter, more energetic time — though here too,
every body is different.

This article describes general patterns. It is not medical advice; if you have
questions about your cycle, see a doctor.

## How the follicular phase can feel

As the phase progresses, many people notice more energy, a clearer head and a desire
to take things on. It is a natural moment to start something new or move a little
more actively — if that suits you on the day.

## Movement in this phase

Does your body feel energetic? Then this can be a nice moment for some more vigorous
movement. But movement is not an obligation and not compensation — listen to what
feels good. A calm walk counts just as much.

## Nutrition that fits

Fresh, light meals feel good to many people in this phase. Think vegetables, whole
grains and fermented foods like sauerkraut. It is about care, not rules or numbers.
