# Eating around ovulation

Around ovulation, many people are at an energy peak. Fibre-rich and colourful food
fits that nicely and supports your body in a natural way. As always: these are
suggestions, not prescriptions.

This is general information, not medical or nutritional advice.

## Fibre

Whole grains, legumes, flaxseed and vegetables provide fibre that supports your
digestion. A handful of seeds over your salad or yoghurt is an easy way to add a little
extra fibre.

## Colour on your plate

Colourful vegetables and fruit — think berries, peppers, broccoli and leafy greens —
bring a mix of nutrients. Variety and colour are a pleasant guide, far easier than
counting.

## At your energy peak

Feeling full of energy? After a more active day you can calmly top up with a nourishing
meal that includes some protein. Eating to support your energy is care, not
compensation.
